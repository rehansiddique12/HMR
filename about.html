About

Welcome to
Haji M.Rajiq & Sons

Haji M.Rajiq & Sons is a trusted and experienced engineering firm dedicated to delivering reliable and efficient water solutions across industrial, agricultural, and public sectors. Built on decades of hands-on expertise and a strong legacy of workmanship, the company specializes in complete tubewell drilling, deep well installation, and advanced pumping systems designed to meet diverse operational needs. From survey and geophysical logging to the installation of turbine, submersible, and centrifugal pumps, every project is executed with precision, durability, and performance in mind. The company serves a wide range of applications including industries, buildings, farms, poultry operations, drip irrigation systems, fish farms, and public institutions such as schools and hospitals. By combining proven engineering practices with modern technology,

Haji M.Rajiq & Sons focuses on delivering sustainable, cost-effective, and long-lasting water management solutions. Driven by a commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction, the company continues to empower growth, strengthen infrastructure, and support communities with dependable water systems built to stand the test of time.
